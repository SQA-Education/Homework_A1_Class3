🎧 1. Listening — Making an Appointment

Tom: I want to make an appointment.

Sara: Okay, we have available next Wednesday at noon, or 2 o'clock. What do you prefer?

Tom: Wednesday at noon, please.

Sara: Can I have your name?

Tom: My name is Tom.

Sara: And your phone number?

Tom: 305-976-1234.

Sara: Perfect. You have an appointment next Wednesday at noon. See you then!

Tom: Thank you!
